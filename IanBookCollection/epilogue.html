EPILOGUE

Home

Several days later, Rowan opened the mysterious book one final time.

The city lights of Everhafen glowed softly beyond his window.

The river reflected a thousand lanterns.

The evening bells echoed somewhere in the distance.

For a moment, he simply watched the blank pages.

Then, slowly, new words appeared.

📖
Dear traveler,

You have arrived.

Rowan smiled.

The sentence felt strange.

Because the truth was that he didn't feel finished.

There were still places he wanted to see.

Still roads he wanted to walk.

Still questions he didn't understand.

And somehow, that no longer frightened him.

🏮

The next line appeared beneath the first.

You searched for a city.
You searched for a destination.
You searched for certainty.

More words followed.

But home was never hidden inside a place.

Rowan's eyes lingered on the sentence.

Then the final message appeared.

Home was the lantern beside you.
✨

Suddenly he understood.

The old traveler.

The mysterious book.

The road.

The bridge.

The companion who had met him beneath the lantern.

None of them had been leading him toward a city.

They had been teaching him something.

Something he had spent years trying to find.

You never ask who is carrying the lantern.

For so long Rowan believed home was waiting somewhere far away.

Beyond mountains.

Beyond trains.

Beyond maps.

Beyond every place he had ever known.

But home wasn't waiting at the end of the road.

It wasn't hidden inside Everhafen.

It wasn't hidden inside a destination at all.

🌙

Home was the feeling of not walking alone anymore.

Home was laughter shared beside a river.

Home was someone noticing when you arrived.

Home was a lantern glowing beside you in the dark.

Home was being expected.

Being remembered.

Being welcomed.

And perhaps most importantly...

Home was allowing yourself to believe you deserved those things.

The End

✦


The story will still be here tomorrow.
